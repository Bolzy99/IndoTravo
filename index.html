Founded by SHALLY, a passionate globetrotter with over 15 enriching years living amidst the stunning landscapes and vibrant culture of Switzerland, INDOTRAVO was born from a deep love for exploration.

"My years in Switzerland were truly transformative, revealing the profound beauty and unique experiences travel can offer. I believe every journey holds the potential for lifelong memories and meaningful connections, no matter where in the world you choose to explore."

Driven by a desire to share this magic, INDOTRAVO is dedicated to curating exceptional and personalized travel experiences, not just for Switzerland, but across the globe. We aim to immerse you in the essence of each destination, ensuring every moment is extraordinary.

What sets us apart is our genuine commitment to understanding your unique travel dreams and crafting journeys that exceed expectations. From meticulously curated itineraries to insider tips and 24/7 support, we go above and beyond to make your adventure seamless and unforgettable.
